Our Book Review

Disney’s Ali Baba and the 40 thieves

Mickey mouse’s adaptation of ‘Ali baba and the 40 thieves’ a much loved folk tale based on a hard working wood chopper who comes across treasure hidden in a cave belonging to the 40 thieves. Ali baba gets involved in the treasure and is then chased down by the thieves. An adventure leading to the capture of the thieves and Ali baba’s financial circumstance changing.

Walt Disney’s 101 Dalmations

101 dalmations takes you on an exciting family adventure. Hang onto your hats, this book is filled with action and excitement. A fun read for all.

Disney’s The little Mermaid and Spot

A sequel to ‘The Little mermaid’ classic. This book takes you on a sea adventure where Ariel and friends overcome a difficulty with the help of their friend Spot, a very large dolphin. This book teaches the importance of friendship and the acceptance of others.

Walt Disney’s The Jungle Book

A timeless classic based in the jungle where a boy is raised by wolves. He must overcome his limitations as a human living in the wild and survive through the hardships he faces. Mowgli has won the hearts of millions over the years and is must read.

Disnep’s The little mermaid

A children’s story book with the heroin Ariel, a mermaid and princess who’s wish to be human comes true but at a price. This book teaches the importance of friendship and bravery.

Calm Parents, Happy Kids By Laura Markham

A calming and powerful approach to raising your kids with the parents mentality being priority. This book teaches you that parenting does not to be a difficult life challenge but can be relaxing and enjoying when done right. Diverting parents away from punishments and manipulation this book teaches parents on the soft approach to parenting and child development.

British National Formulary

Often called the ‘Pharmacist’s bible’ the BNF is a complete selection of information on medicines for health care professionals. A brief overview is given at the start of each section on medical conditions. Then detailed information is available on the prescribing, monitoring, and administering of an endless number of drugs along with their side effects and the interactions that may occur when administered.

Walt Disney’s Cinderella

This remake of the classic ‘Cinderella’ is a much loved story about a poor girl raised by her evil stepmother and 2 step sisters who make her life miserable. A bit of magic, a glass shoe and a change of fortune leads to a happy ending for Cinderella and her animal friends.

Walt Disney’s Brer Rabbit Gets Tricked

Based on the much loved classic ‘The tortoise and the hare’ this book comes with a twist and exciting adventure where the tortoise tricks the rabbit with the help of his friends. This leads to the rabbit losing faith but when he gets into a dangerous situation time after time, he realises he is actually good at escaping and is quite smart. This book is about confidence and bravery.

Disney’s The Aristocats Get Into Mischief

A story about a rich old lady who a much loved and cat and 3 spoilt kittens. When a new ‘street’ cat is introduced to the family chaos follows and a new adventure unfolds. Read about the posh ‘Aristocats’ and their new adventure.

Walt Disney’s Winnie the Pooh and Tigger Too

Winnie the pooh, a sweet and kind bear meets his friends for the first time. This honey loving bear and friends face some difficulties but always have fun and remain loyal to one another. A much loved story book loved by all.
